About TipBetter

TipBetter determines the best tip to give by subtracting the taxation from your bill before calculating the tip.

Start by entering your bill and then the tax.

Next, you can use the slider to choose the percent of tip to add.

Press the refresh button to reset the bill, tax, and tip fields to zero.

Press the color button to change the background color of your tip calculator. Press and hold this button to return to the default coloration of TipBetter.

Press the heart button to save your current color scheme. Next time you load TipBetter, the background color will be the same!

Press the back button to erase your most recent keypad entry. Press and hold this button to reset the active section (bill, tax, or tip) to zero.
